Where I've Been

So, let's be honest. You probably stumbled upon this site by mistake. But you're here now, so we might as well make the best of it, right? If the huge title didn't tip you off well enough, I'm Mack. Mack Quickley. And if my understanding of the universe is anywhere near correct, I shouldn't exist. To you, anyway. Because I'm not from YOUR universe. You see, I'm what I now like to call a "shifter." That is to say, I'm able to move between realities - what you would call dimensions.

Now, I know what you're thinking. And surprisingly, yes, I too had to sit through 4 years of a half-wit narcissist at the helm of my country. As many peoples of many realities have come to believe, dimensions run parallel to one another. Model-wise, the truth is entirely more complex but, in a nutshell, the closer the realities are to one another, the more similar they are.

Even so, there are an infinite number of realities, and shifters are folks who are apparently allergic to the boundaries that typically separate them. And while that may sound cool, imagine bleeding into a completely new reality - whilst entirely unaware, I might add - while preparing for raunchy intercourse with the daughter of the dean of your alma mater, in said dean's office. Let's imagine that in your origin reality, that dean was a by-the-book nine to five man, who wouldn't be caught dead coming back to his office after leaving for the day. Skip ahead to laying across a beautiful mahogany desk butt-naked thinking that the person about to open the door and get an eye-full of your impressive junk would be a beautiful blonde bombshell and not a slightly overweight wannabe golfer with a sweating problem.

It's always the subtle differences that get you in the end.

Except it wasn't me that got got. Thing about those first few shifts, is that you never stay in any one dimension for too long. So in reality, I had only been a passerby participant in ruining the college career of another Mack - one of my infinite "alters." And, unless he's a shifter like I happen to be, his memory will automatically include the information that I collected while taking his place. Of course, if he was a shifter, he would have switched places with me or any other Mack who arbitrarily abandoned his (or her?) reality.
